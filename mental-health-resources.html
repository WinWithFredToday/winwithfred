Mental Health & Crisis Resources

You don’t have to figure this out alone. Below are free, confidential resources for crisis support, mental health help, and more — for the U.S. and internationally.

If You Need Help Right Now

Fastest Ways to Get Support

988
Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
Call or text 988 — 24/7, English & Spanish. Free and confidential.
Text HOME
Crisis Text Line
Text HOME to 741741 — 24/7 text-based support. No talking required.
988, Press 1
Veterans Crisis Line
Call 988 and press 1, or text 838255. For veterans and their families.

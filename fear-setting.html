Decision & Action

Fear Setting Tool

Define the worst case. Calculate the cost of inaction. Then decide.

What are you afraid to do?

Name the thing you keep putting off or avoiding out of fear. Be specific.

Column 1 — Define the worst case

What are the worst things that could realistically happen if you do this? List up to 5.

Column 2 — Prevent or repair each

For each worst case: how could you prevent it, or recover if it happened?

Column 3 — Cost of inaction

If you do NOTHING and stay exactly where you are, what does that cost you in 6 months? 1 year? 3 years?
